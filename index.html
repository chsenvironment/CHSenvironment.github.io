The Call

Would you fight for your life if you had to?

Be prepared, because soon enough you will have to.

Never before has the earth undergone such drastic environmental damages.

Catastrophe is looming!

Most of us fail to acknowledge this issue properly and do our due diligence.

However, you can still stop the destruction of our planet.

We alone can avert disaster by turning ourselves and others into environmentally responsible people.

To solve our global crisis, we need the collective efforts of everyone.

Not just you and me, but our friends, family, and acquaintances must take action.

Ladies and gentlemen, this is a call to action, a call for rescue, and a call for survival.

Save our beautiful earth.

- written by Sai K Raja
