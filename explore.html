Corvallis, Oregon

Organizations and Opportunities

There are several organizations and opportunities in Corvallis that can lead you to become a more environmentally responsible person.

Lots of Water + Fabric = Shirt

Local Clothing

It takes about 700 gallon of water to make one T-shirt. To eliminate the usage of such large amounts of water, buy used clothing. This will reduce the number of new clothes manufactured and transported to corvallis. At these stores, you can sell or donate/buy used clothing

  • Goodwill
  • The Arc Thrift Store
  • Second Glance Annex
  • Mother Goose Resale
  • Peak Sports Annex
  • re•volve
  • Runway Fashion Exchange

Contribute to Your Community

These organizations provide great opportunities for you to help reduce your negative impact on the environment

  • Corvallis Environmental Center
  • Corvallis Sustainability Coalition
  • Seeds for the Sol

    Offset your carbon footprint by donating a certain amount based on your miles traveled; helps schools and low income homes go solar.

  • Friends of Family Farmers

    “Friends of Family Farmers works daily to promote and protect socially responsible agriculture in Oregon
